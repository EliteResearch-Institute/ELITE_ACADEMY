Learn the foundation of accounting with this quick lesson. Understand assets, liabilities, and how to prepare simple financial statements.

📘 Lesson 1: What is Accounting?

Accounting is the process of recording, summarizing, and reporting financial transactions of a business.

📊 Key Concepts:

  • Assets: What the business owns (cash, equipment)
  • Liabilities: What the business owes (loans, debts)
  • Equity: Owner's investment in the business

🧮 Simple Example:

If a business has ₦100,000 cash and owes ₦40,000:

Equity = ₦60,000
